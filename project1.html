What is Chibi Champions?

Chibi Champions is a chaotic third person tower-defense/brawler game where you can play as three adorable, yet mutated animals trying to survive against rogue scienents that have released an onslaught of enemies upon you. As tortured lab subjects in a dystopian world, you must defend the cure from being destroyed alongside your newfound friends.

  • Build powerful towers to assist you and protect yourself from enemies.
  • Gather coins to upgrade your towers and unlock cool new abilities.
  • With three characters to choose from, each with their own arsenal of towers and upgrades to build, see which character fits your playstyle.
