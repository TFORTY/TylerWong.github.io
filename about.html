About Me

Hey there! My name is Tyler.

I'm a game developer and designer with experience in systemic design content design and UX/UI design. I love creating immersive and interactive experiences and always try to approach game design with a player-first mindset. Currently I am developing games/passion projects in my freetime, participating in game jams, refining my skills and learning new things!.

For the past 4 years, I have continued my academic journey at Ontario Tech University in Oshawa, ON. Forming a student-run studio by the name of Raw Chicken Productions, I have gained experience designing several different games and applications.

Originally starting out focusing on gameplay programming, I have found a passion for game design and crafting intricate systems and experiences for players to enjoy. Learning about all the pillars of game development have given me insight in the production pipeline and I have used these learnings to lead the design on several projects at Raw Chicken Productions, desigining UI for games, and leading several projects as well throughout the years.

I love working in an environment where team members can bounce ideas off one another and are on the same wavelength. The feeling of understanding everyone's ideas and drive to deliver the best quality product is a feeling like no other and exemplifies what game developmemnt is all about.
